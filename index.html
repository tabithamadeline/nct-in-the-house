About Us

Neo Culture Technology

NCT (엔시티), an acronym for ‘Neo Culture Technology’, is a new boy group concept. They are described as having an unlimited number of members. The group currently has 23 members and consists of Taeyong, Taeil, Johnny, Yuta, Kun, Doyoung, Ten, Jaehyun, WinWin, Jungwoo, Lucas, Mark, Xiaojun, Hendery, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, YangYang, Shotaro, Sungchan, Chenle, and Jisung. NCT promotes in different sub-units. The current sub-units are: NCT U, NCT 127, NCT Dream and WayV. They are under SM Entertainment.
